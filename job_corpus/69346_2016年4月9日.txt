根據會計準則
能被分類為financial liabilities的條件有4個
只要滿足其中一個就可以了
1.Contractual obligation to deliver cash or another financial asset to another entity
2.Contractual obligation to exchange financial assets or financial liabilities with another entity under conditions that are potentially unfavourable to the entity
3.The entity is or may be obligated to deliver a variable number of the entity's own equity instruments
4. May be settled other than by exchange of a fixed amount of cash or another F/A for a fixed number of the entity's own equity instruments

在我看來interest rate swaps不能滿足任何一個條件
首先是1跟2. interest rate swaps是cash flow的交換而不是單方面送錢. 而且不太會是potentially unfavourable,如果是unfavourable那為何又要簽這個合鉉
第3跟第4點也不對, 整個合約都跟equity無關

我感覺第2點是比較有可能是答案
但我還是想不通
這合約明明是用來給分散利率風險的
為何會是unfavourable呢?
