大家好，我是麥克，目前在一家外商擔任行銷副總監。快到了年底轉職潮，前陣子有許多人詢問我離職信如何撰寫，因此寫了這篇離職信撰寫攻略，直接提供了完整的模板給需要的人參考，祝大家都能找到心儀的工作。

撰寫離職信無需過於複雜，
掌握幾大要素，輕鬆撰寫離職信

提出離職意向
告知離職日期
感謝公司栽培
協助交接事宜
幾句祝福話語

1. 提出離職意向
離職信簡單扼要即可，離職原因及細節可以當面與主管或HR説明，無需在離職信中寫出來。

#範例：Please accept this letter as a formal notice that I am resigning from my position as [Position Title] at [Company Name].

2. 告知離職日期
確保預告離職日期，除了口頭與主管約定好，一定也要有郵件記錄。如工作一年以上，最好能30日前預告，給公司足夠的時間刊登職缺。

#範例：My last day will be [Desired Last Working Day].

3. 感謝公司栽培
工作後會發現職場真的很小，到了最後一刻也該留下好印象，記得感謝主管的栽培。除了給主管及HR的離職信外，建議在最後一天寫一份離職感謝函給工作中遇到的夥伴們。

#範例：Thank you for the opportunity to allow me to work for [Company Name]. I truly enjoyed my time here, and am grateful for all the guidance and support given to me. I‘ve learned so much from you and the team.  

4. 協助交接事宜
任職期間的優異表現，若因沒好好交接而留下不好的印象就太可惜了！提出離職後，主動協助後續交接事宜是種美德。完善交接文件，不止繼任者會感謝你，還能為你在公司留下好聲譽。

#範例：I would be happy to do anything I could to help with the transition, including wrapping up my responsibilities and training other team members. Please let me know how I can be of further assistance.

5. 幾句祝福話語
郵件的結尾寫一些祝福的話吧！

#範例：
Thank you again for the opportunity. I wish you and <Company Name> all the best.
I wish you and the company continued success in the future.

#完整範例：
Hi Sara,

Please accept this letter as a formal notice that I am resigning from my position as Marketing Manager at Microsoft. My last day will be 11/30/2020.

Thank you for the opportunity to allow me to work for Microsoft. I truly enjoyed my time here, and am grateful for all the guidance and support given to me. I‘ve learned so much from you and the team.  

I would be happy to do anything I could to help with the transition, including wrapping up my responsibilities and training other team members. Please let me know how I can be of further assistance.

I wish you and the company continued success in the future.

Sincerely,
Coach Mike
