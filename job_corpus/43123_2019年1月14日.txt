什麼是Au pair 呢？ 

Au pair 在台灣代辦的說法叫美國安親保母。
意思就是呢，家庭提供你食宿零用錢還有部分學分費以及體驗當地生活與文化，而au apir 每週會最多會有45個小時工作。
工作內容就是以照顧小孩為主，例如接送小孩上學（會依家庭小孩年紀工作內容會有不一樣）

這個計畫非常適合大學剛畢業的新鮮人，可以出國走走，因為當你去修學分的時候，會遇到很多來自世界各地的人，他們會分享他們的夢想，生活方式。
對於自己本身也是一種不一樣的體驗！！


我目前自己目前是休士頓的Au apir，前些日子遇到這個夏威夷媽媽❤️❤️自己心裡想夏威夷耶！！真的很心動想自己過去，但是因為男朋友在德州，所以只好忍痛了！

以下是轟家他們給的基本資料（媽媽希望小孩可以講中文，但是如果你會其他語言例如：法文、德文、那也是很棒喔）

美國住家保姆 (Au Pair) 
開始期間: 2019/03 (提早可以)

我們在尋找保姆 (au pair) 來加入我們在美國夏威夷鄉村的家庭，與她分享我們的文化和生活。雖然夫妻兩人都是美國人，但我的家人來自台灣（我會中文）而我先生的家人來自德國（他會德文和法文)。 家裡主要講英文。除了英文能力中等以上，我們在尋找有中文，德文，和/或法文能力的人，以形成真正的多語種家庭。我們現在有一個兩歲的兒子 而打算有更多孩子。
We are looking to select an au pair to join our family in a rural part of Hawaii, United States, and to share with her our culture and life here. Though my husband and I are both American, my family is from Taiwan (I speak Mandarin) and my husband's family is from Germany (he speaks German and French). Although we speak English in the house, we are looking for someone who can speak as many of these languages (in addition to English) as possible, to form a truly multi-lingual household. We currently have one 2-year old son, who is a true joy for us, and we plan to continue growing our family. 

It is very important that our au pair truly enjoys being around young children and has experience actively engaging with them. We need someone who is trustworthy, energetic, patient, and loving to care for our son (and possibly a new baby). Safety of our son is the most important responsibility. Other responsibilities include cleaning up indoor/outdoor play areas and assisting in cooking. Reading and doing a variety of projects are very important in our household, so we are looking for  an au pair who encourages both. We would like someone who enjoys a quiet/rural lifestyle and does not: smoke, do drugs, drink excessively, party, or gossip (privacy is important to us).
